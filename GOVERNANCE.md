# Governance

## Model

Fairvisor Edge follows a maintainer-led governance model.

Maintainers are responsible for:

- technical direction
- release approvals
- security response coordination
- project health and contributor experience

## Decision Making

- Routine decisions are made in pull requests.
- Architectural changes should include a short design rationale in the PR description.
- If maintainers disagree, the final decision is made by the lead maintainer for the affected area.

## Becoming a Maintainer

A contributor may be invited as a maintainer after sustained, high-quality work in one or more areas:

- runtime policy engine
- CLI and tooling
- tests and operational docs

The existing maintainer group approves new maintainers by consensus.

## Removal or Inactivity

Maintainers who are inactive for an extended period may be moved to emeritus status.

Access and roles may be adjusted for project security and continuity.
